  2. Travel task - web service

    The goal Many students and young people aspire to travel and see the world, but often face financial constraints associated with high accommodation expenses during their journeys. Traditional hotels and apartments are often unaffordable for this audience, limiting their travel and change of environment opportunities. Our mission is to create a web service that helps young individuals and students realize their travel dreams by providing affordable lodging and meals in new places. Through this platform, we aim to promote budget-friendly travel and foster cultural exchange on a global scale.

  3. The redesign for a tattoo studio based in Destin, Florida.

    The design of the existing site looks outdated, not very user-friendly, has low-quality photos and lacks some important information for customers (for example, an indicative price list). Therefore, the goal of the project was to create a modern and user-friendly design that could attract potential customers. The target audience is young and middle-aged people who are interested in tattoos as a way of self-expression, and also have an aesthetic or symbolic meaning for them. Before starting the redesign, research and analysis of competitors was carried out. It helped to understand which successful solutions can be used for our project.
